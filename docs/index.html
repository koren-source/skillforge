Building a $12,000,000 Business for a Stranger

Frameworks

Supply vs Demand Constraint Analysis

Diagnose whether business growth is limited by demand or supply before deciding what to fix.

UGC Content Loop System

Incentivize customers to post content, test organically, take winners with a 5-second CTA, run as paid ads. 20–30 variations per week.

Damaging Admissions Framework

Build credibility by frontloading all negatives before benefits. They want it — they just don’t believe it.

Key Numbers
1.4:1 LTV:CAC — unsustainable, requires 6-month breakeven
$100K/month ad ceiling — creative constraint, not budget
85% from single channel — dangerous platform concentration
Key Quote
"The easiest unlock is usually creative, not money."
